Projects

FoundMyFitness is a web platform focused on the science of nutrition, genetics, and cognitive and physical wellbeing. It features articles, interviews and research, empowering users to make informed decisions about their health and wellness.

Skedaddle is a travel itinerary generator. It consumes several APIs to create a custom itinerary with parks, restaurants, airports, museums, maps and more, and uses AI to generate a recommended day-by-day play-by-play for your trip.

Momentum is a personal assistant that helps users build routines and manage their nutrition, fitness, reading, and job search with stats and AI-powered insights for personalized guidance and motivation.

Year Zero is an interactive web experience set in a dystopian future. Users navigate government terminals, uncover hidden interactions, and piece together lost history through puzzles and fragmented records.
